📐 Key Formulas
Support
P(A ∪ B) = count(A,B) / N
How often items appear together across all transactions
Confidence
P(B|A) = P(A∪B) / P(A)
Given A is bought, probability that B is also bought
Lift
Lift = Confidence / P(B)
Lift > 1 means A and B are positively correlated
